Why UFVWealth?

UFVWealth is the official platform for UFVCoin, giving UFV students, staff, and alumni a secure way to manage their coins, stake for rewards, and engage with the UFV community. Unlike traditional apps, UFVWealth puts your benefits and community first.

Key Features

  • Purchase UFVCoin securely using your UFV credentials
  • Earn coins through volunteering, academic achievements, and participation
  • Spend coins on tuition, parking, cafeteria, scholarships, and raffles
  • Track your coin balance, staking, and rewards in your personal UFVWealth dashboard
  • Learn investing basics and manage your UFVCoin responsibly
