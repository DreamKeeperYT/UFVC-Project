UFVWealth & UFVCoin Tutorial

Step-by-step guide to start investing, earning, and using UFVCoin responsibly.

1. Sign Up

Create a UFVWealth account with your UFV email (@student.ufv.ca or staff). Only UFV stakeholders can access UFVCoin.

2. Fund Your Account

Deposit funds into your UFVWealth account. For example, $100 allows you to buy 99 UFVCoin (1% platform fee supports the community).

3. Purchase UFVCoin

Buy coins to pay for tuition, parking, cafeteria, library, or campus events. Track all transactions in real-time on your dashboard.

4. Earn Rewards & Stake

Get bonus UFVCoin through volunteering, campus engagement, or academic excellence (A+ reward system). Long-term staking increases coin value.

5. Spending UFVCoin

Use your coins for scholarships, raffles, or campus purchases. Ethical investing ensures benefits stay within the UFV community.

6. Security & Compliance

All transactions are encrypted, transparent, and follow Canadian crypto regulations. Wallets are tied to UFV credentials to prevent misuse.

7. Future Opportunities

Participate in future reward programs, inter-university collaborations, and community projects while staying ethically invested.
