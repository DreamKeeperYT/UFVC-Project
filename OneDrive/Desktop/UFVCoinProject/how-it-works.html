How UFVCoin Works

Invest, earn, and spend your UFVCoin securely and ethically.

Steps to Use UFVCoin

  1. Sign up with your UFV email account
  2. Purchase coins via UFVWealth platform
  3. Use coins for tuition, parking, food, library books
  4. Earn additional coins by volunteering or achieving academic excellence
  5. Track progress and manage coins in your dashboard

Investment Benefits

  • Long-term staking opportunities
  • Community-driven rewards
  • Learning money management and crypto investment safely
  • Supporting the UFV community rather than external profit-seeking apps
