Using UFVWealth

Step-by-step guide to safely invest, earn, and manage your UFVCoin while contributing to the UFV community.

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your UFVWealth account using your UFV credentials (@student.ufv.ca or staff account). Only verified UFV stakeholders can access the platform.

Step 2: Buy UFVCoin

Purchase UFVCoin securely through the platform. Remember: 1% goes to UFVWealth for platform maintenance and community initiatives.

Step 3: Spend Coins

  • Tuition and fees
  • Parking and transportation
  • Cafeteria and library purchases
  • Raffles and scholarship opportunities

Step 4: Earn Extra Coins

  • Volunteer on campus and community projects
  • Engage in campus events
  • Achieve academic excellence (A+ reward system)

Step 5: Track Your Progress

Use your UFVWealth dashboard to monitor:

  • Coin balance
  • Staking progress
  • Recent transactions
  • Rewards and bonuses

Community & Ethical Impact

Every UFVCoin transaction supports UFV scholarships, bursaries, and campus initiatives. By participating, you are learning financial literacy, earning rewards, and strengthening the UFV community — all while avoiding profit-driven traditional investment apps.

On-Campus Transactions

Click to learn how on-campus crypto transactions are managed

To manage on campus transactions, our payment processors would need to consider our coin’s volatility and implement some strategies to complete successful transactions. We would need to implement strategies to calculate and handle payment. Below are a few strategies often used by other big crypto coins like Bitcoin.

Strategies to calculate payment:
Real time exchange conversions

Strategies to handle payment:
Use of a stablecoin
Conversion to fiat currency

Real Time Exchange Conversions

Real time exchange conversion is when payment processors will process the transaction based on the coin’s price at the time of purchase. The payment processor will instantly get the coin’s current market value, lock it in and use that price for the transaction.

For example,
Tim Hortons will have a steep tea priced at $2. Chacha asks to pay with our UFV coin. The employee will select our UFV coin as the selected payment, and the payment processor will instantly get the coin’s current price. The processor will convert $2 to the equivalent UFV coin amount and display that price. The price will be locked in while Chacha pays for the transaction. This is to prevent price fluctuations while the transaction is being paid for.

Use of a Stablecoin

Stablecoins are coins “being pegged to fiat currencies”(Team, Speed,2025), such as the US or Canadian dollar. After the transaction, the payment processor will immediately convert the UFV coin to a stablecoin. A benefit of using a stablecoin is to allow the merchants/university to receive payment “within the crypto ecosystem”(Team, Speed,2025) while still being safe from the coin’s volatility.

For example,

When Chacha’s payment is received, the UFV coin is immediately converted to a stablecoin. Tim Horton will have received $2 worth of a stablecoin in their account. Now Time hortons doesn’t have to worry about their revenue increasing or decreasing while still holding crypto.

Conversion to Fiat Currency

Similar to stablecoin, the merchant can decide to instead have their revenue be immediately converted to a fiat currency such as Canadian dollars. A UFV merchant or the university itself, may prefer to receive payments in Canadian dollars rather than hold crypto. After the transaction, the payment processor will immediately convert the UFV coin into Canadian dollars.

For example,

When Chacha’s payment is received, the UFV coin is immediately converted to Canadian dollars. Tim Horton will have received $2 Canadian dollars in their account. Now Time hortons doesn’t have to worry about their revenue increasing or decreasing due to crypto prices and holds their revenue in Canadian dollars.

These methods allow students and staff to use our UFV coin for payment while minimizing the impact of the coin’s volatility.
