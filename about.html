About UFVWealth & UFVCoin

UFVCoin (UFVC) is UFV’s official crypto currency, and UFVWealth is the platform that powers it. Together, they allow students, staff, and alumni to invest, earn rewards, and strengthen our UFV community ethically and responsibly.

Our Mission

UFVWealth is designed to be community-first, ethical, and educational. Unlike traditional investment apps, our goal is not profit but to empower UFV stakeholders with tools to learn investing, earn rewards, and contribute to the campus community.

UFVWealth Platform Highlights

  • Secure login with UFV credentials (@student.ufv.ca or staff accounts)
  • Buy and manage UFVCoin for campus services: tuition, parking, cafeteria, library, and more
  • Real-time dashboard tracking for coin balance, staking, and rewards
  • Earn extra coins via volunteering, engagement, and academic achievements (A+ reward system!)
  • Transparent platform fee (1%) supports maintenance, scholarships, and campus initiatives
  • Ethical, community-oriented, and safe — unlike traditional apps that prioritize profit

UFVCoin Features

  • Exclusive cryptocurrency for UFV stakeholders
  • Used for scholarships, bursaries, raffles, and campus perks
  • Encourages financial literacy and responsible investing among students
  • Ethical and secure — community benefits first
  • Future potential for inter-university collaborations or campus events

Why UFVWealth & UFVCoin?

UFVWealth isn’t just another financial app. By combining UFVCoin with a secure, easy-to-use platform, users can participate in ethical investing, gain rewards, and support the UFV community! All while learning valuable money management skills. Traditional investment programs can’t offer this unique combination of education, rewards, and community impact.
