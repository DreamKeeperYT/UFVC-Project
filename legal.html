Legal & Security

UFVWealth and UFVCoin prioritize safety, privacy, and compliance — giving UFV stakeholders peace of mind.

Security Features

  • Encrypted transactions for privacy and safety
  • Fraud detection and misuse prevention
  • Verified participation for UFV students, staff, and alumni only
  • Tax-compliant usage for campus purchases

Quick Legal Tips

  • Keep your UFVCoin account secure — never share passwords
  • Check your dashboard for transaction history
  • Use coins only for authorized campus services
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

FAQs

Campus transactions are managed under UFV’s accounting; regular tax rules apply only for external exchanges.

Only verified UFV students, staff, and alumni with UFVWealth accounts can access UFVCoin.

UFVWealth has secure backups; contact support to recover lost coins safely.
